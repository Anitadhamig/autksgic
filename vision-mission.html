OUR MISSION

impart sound education with English as a medium of instruction. To attempt at the development of every individual child taking into consideration the mental, physical, social and spiritual needs of the child, so that the child may grow up as responsible citizen. To inculcate values of attitudes and habits, which will help in the creation of new social order, based on human dignity, equality of opportunity. The school is famous in Uttarkashi, for providing Secondary and Senior Secondary education based on the CBSE pattern.

OUR VISION

The school believes that education should lead the students to a practical, confident and successful life and that every child has the potential to be the light of the world and our endeavor is to see the budding children as the dream kids and pride achievers in the forthcoming days.
